            <h1>Welcome to the ICZN</h1><p><img src="/assets/6387da6021/Capture__ResizedImageWzExOCwxNjdd.PNG" alt="Capture" width="118" height="167" class="left ss-htmleditorfield-file image" title="BZN2019 cover">The International Commission on Zoological Nomenclature (ICZN) acts as adviser and arbiter for the zoological community by generating and disseminating information on the correct use of the scientific names of animals. The ICZN is responsible for producing the International Code of Zoological Nomenclature - a set of rules for the naming of animals and the resolution of nomenclatural problems.</p><h2>Quick Links</h2><ul>

                                <u>First contribution to the List of Available Names in Zoology –- Species- and Genus- Parts of Phylum Rotifera</u>
                            
                            <p>The International Commission of Zoological Nomenclature has adopted as <em>Parts</em> of the <em>List of Available Names in Zoology</em> both Candidate Parts of Phylum Rotifera, compiled by Jersabek et al. (2018a, b). The Ruling was published on the <em>Bulletin of Zoological Nomenclature </em>as Opinion 2430, on 30 April 2019 (link here, open access: ). These Parts are the first to be added to the <em>List </em>.</p><p>The ICZN would like to thank the LAN Committees as well as the international community of rotiferologists for this commendable effort.</p><p>In order to commemorate this occasion, the cover of this year's <em>Bulletin</em> features the winning photograph in a rotifer picture competition, of <em>Keratella serrulata</em> (Ehrenberg, 1838) © Michael Plewka, plingfactory.de. This is the first time the Bulletin has featured a photograph on its front cover.</p><p>PDFs of both Parts can be downloaded from archive.org (below), or from the <a href="node/40612">ICZN website.</a></p><p><a href="https://archive.org/details/LANCandidatePartGeneraRotifera">Candidate Part Phylum Rotifera, genus-group names established before 1 January 2000, compiled by Jersabek et al., 2018</a>.</p><p><a href="https://archive.org/details/LANCandidatePartSpeciesRotifera">Candidate Part Phylum Rotifera, species-group names established before 1 January 2000, compiled by Jersabek et al., 2018</a>.</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
